
Cheetah: A large spotted cat found in Africa and parts of Asia. It is the fastest animal on land.

Tiger: A very large solitary cat with a yellow-brown coat striped with black, native to the forests of Asia but becoming increasingly rare.

Lion: A large tawny-colored cat that lives in prides, found in Africa and northwestern India. The male has a flowing shaggy mane and takes little part in hunting, which is done cooperatively by the females.

Leopard: A large, solitary cat that has a yellowish-brown or brown coat with black spots and usually hunts at night, widespread in the forests of Africa and southern Asia.

Jaguar: A large heavily built cat that has a yellowish-brown coat with black spots, found mainly in the dense forests of Central and South America.
